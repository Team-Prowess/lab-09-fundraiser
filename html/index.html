Thank you for supporting music education for the youth of Portland!

PROWUS -- Portland Rock on With Us -- is a non-profit, 501(c)3 organization that provides scholarships to music students age 8 to 18 in the greater Portland Metro area who are pursuing extra-curricular music education of all genres, including Classical, Jazz, Rock, etc. Operated by a volunteer board, PROWUS builds its grant fund with proceeds from donations, merchandise sales and fundraising events.
